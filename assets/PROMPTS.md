# Image-generation prompts

These are the prompts used with ChatGPT's built-in image-generation model.
Each later campaign asset used the preceding approved image as its visual
reference. The first banner draft used “OPEN SOURCE”; the final correction
replaces that claim pending an owner-selected repository licence.

> The “OPEN SOURCE” wording below is retained only as historical prompt
> provenance. Do not reuse those superseded draft lines unless the repository
> owner first adds a licence that grants reuse rights.

## Logo

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: square repository logo and social-media avatar
Primary request: Create an original logo mark for Sunofriend, an open-source music tool that transforms separated audio stems into clean editable MIDI for DAWs.
Subject: one continuous, friendly S-shaped audio waveform that smoothly resolves into a precise piano-roll grid of rectangular MIDI notes; the transformation from organic sound to clean musical structure must read instantly.
Style/medium: minimal flat vector-like logo mark, strong silhouette, balanced negative space, polished contemporary music-technology identity.
Composition/framing: one centered symbol, generous padding, designed to remain legible when cropped into a circular profile image and when displayed at 32 pixels.
Color palette: bright electric cyan and warm coral-orange on a deep midnight-navy background; at most three flat colors.
Constraints: symbol only; no text, no letters, no gradients, no mockup, no 3D, no realistic instruments, no people, no brand or store logos, no watermark. Original design, crisp edges, visually balanced.
```
