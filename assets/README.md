# Sunofriend brand assets

The identity visualises Sunofriend's central transformation: an organic cyan
audio waveform becomes precise coral MIDI blocks while the overall path forms
a friendly `S` silhouette.

## Files

- `brand/sunofriend-logo.png` — square symbol for avatars and small icons.
- `brand/sunofriend-banner-v2.png` — landscape project wordmark and launch banner.
- `social/the-aisle-at-lidl-square-v2.png` — universal feed post.
- `social/the-aisle-at-lidl-story.png` — vertical Story/Status artwork.

Core palette: deep midnight navy, electric cyan and warm coral-orange. Keep the
logo clear of other marks and retain generous padding around the S silhouette.

## Generation

These raster assets were created with ChatGPT's built-in image-generation
model using structured logo and campaign prompts.

The complete structured prompts are preserved in [`PROMPTS.md`](PROMPTS.md).

Prompt set:

1. **Logo:** an original minimal S-shaped audio waveform resolving into a
   piano-roll grid; flat cyan/coral/navy palette; strong small-size silhouette;
   no text or third-party marks.
2. **Banner:** preserve the approved symbol; exact text “SUNOFRIEND”, “FROM AI
   STEMS TO PLAYABLE MIDI” and “MUSIC MAKERS • DAW READY”; wide safe layout.
3. **Square example:** an abstract supermarket aisle made from MIDI lanes;
   exact “THE AISLE AT LIDL” worked-example copy; no retailer branding.
4. **Story example:** recompose the square campaign as a safe-margin 9:16
   layout with “LISTEN TO VERSION 1”.

Text corrections were also made with the built-in image model. No local
compositing or logo substitution was used.
